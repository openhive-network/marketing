By properly incentivizing these supporters (whether monetarily or in kind), we can encourage organic promotion.

Hive’s presence at hackathons is invaluable for engagement, but expanding these events to include marketers and other industry players can broaden our reach. By increasing our event scope, Hive can appeal to a wider audience.

In today’s digital landscape, visibility is everything. if you’re not posting content, you risk disappearing. A robust social media presence, especially on Twitter and LinkedIn, will enable Hive to engage with the web3 communities, showcase accomplishments, and remain in the heads of potential users and investors.
