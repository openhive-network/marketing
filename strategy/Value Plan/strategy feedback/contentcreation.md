Buttcoins:

How Important is content creation and curation in Hive's marketing strategy?

- Content and curation is an essential piece of the puzzle. Quality content and quality engagement will always lead to an expanding user base. Clear, easy to use tools for finding quality posts on Hive will aid greatly.

What types of content should we focus on to attract and retain users?

- All content is valid. Writing, vlogging, blogging, micro blogging, photography, analysis, memes, podcasts, etc.

HIVE should think of itself like the Walmart of Decentralized Social Media, ‘1 stop shopping for all things social media’, Hive has an open invite to all types of creators to come and play with our community created tools, find the tools they like the best and join the discussion of how to make those tools even better.

Retaining quality creators will happen through engagment and support networks for noobs.
